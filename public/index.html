How To Play

A Dinosaur is picked every 24hrs at midnight (UTC: 00:00)

The aim is to guess today's Dinosaur. Use the clues given by the tiles in order to guess the correct answer!

  1. Click the text field.
  2. Guess a Dinosaur by entering it's name, for example: "Tyrannosaurus Rex" and select it from the dropdown.
  3. The results will give you hints on the correct Dinosaur:
    A Green Tile = Correct,
    A Amber Tile = Partially Correct,
    ⬇️ ⬆️ With arrows, it also indicates if the answer property is above or below your guess.
    A Red Tile = Incorrect
